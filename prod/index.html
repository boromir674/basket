What is Elo?

Elo is a strength rating that updates after each game based on result and opponent strength.

Higher Elo means stronger recent performance relative to league peers.

Use trend and deltas to spot momentum, overperformance, or decline.

Formula: E_new = E_old + K * (S - E_expected), where E_expected = 1 / (1 + 10^((opp - E_old)/400)).

Here S is actual outcome (1 win, 0.5 draw, 0 loss), K is rating sensitivity.
